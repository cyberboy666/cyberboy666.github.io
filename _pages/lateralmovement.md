# lateral movement

lateral movement is a semi-irregular club night showcasing left-field, experimental dance music and light with a focus on the alternative and unexpected. Lateral Movement events are infrequent, curated, and mostly undocumented. Come for the music / stay in the moment / letâ€™s enjoy the journey together.

for the last couple of years iv been working with an amazing crew to put on these experimental club nights in wellington, new zealand - each installment we try to do something a bit different with the visuals and stage design - most recently building an addressable led-light forest on the stage!
